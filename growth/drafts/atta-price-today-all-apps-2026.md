## Why JioMart Is the Cheapest App for Branded Atta Right Now

JioMart wins on Aashirvaad 5kg by a significant margin — Rs 189 versus Rs 228 on BigBasket and Rs 242 on Swiggy Instamart. That is a **21% saving** over the most expensive option.

The reason comes down to sourcing and strategy. JioMart operates through Reliance Retail's massive physical supply chain, which includes direct procurement relationships with FMCG brands like ITC (maker of Aashirvaad). Lower intermediary costs translate directly into lower shelf prices.

JioMart also uses branded staples like atta as **loss-leader products** — they price them aggressively to pull new users in, then cross-sell other categories. If you are buying only atta, this works entirely in your favour.

**One caveat:** JioMart does not always offer 10-minute delivery. If you have run out of atta tonight at 9 PM, Blinkit or Zepto will serve you faster — but you will pay a Rs 51–53 premium for that convenience.

## When to Choose BigBasket for Atta: The Private Label Advantage

BigBasket may not win on Aashirvaad pricing, but it offers something none of the other apps do: **BB Royal 5kg at Rs 155**.

BB Royal is BigBasket's in-house brand and one of the better-regarded private label attas in the market. At Rs 155 versus Rs 189 for Aashirvaad on JioMart, you are saving Rs 34 per 5kg bag — roughly **18% cheaper** than the best branded price available anywhere.

If your household is comfortable with private label flour — and many are, since BB Royal has a strong reputation for consistent quality — BigBasket becomes the clear winner on value.

**Choose BigBasket when:**
- You are flexible on brand and want the lowest price per kg
- You have a scheduled delivery preference and plan purchases in advance
- You are already meeting the Rs 300 minimum order with other groceries

## 10kg vs 5kg Pack: Which Gives Better Value for Atta?

Most platforms list the 5kg pack as the standard option, but if your family uses more than 5kg a month, the **10kg pack almost always offers a lower per-kg rate**.

Using JioMart's Aashirvaad 5kg price of Rs 189, the per-kg rate is **Rs 37.80/kg**. Aashirvaad 10kg packs typically price around Rs 340–360 on most platforms, bringing the per-kg cost down to **Rs 34–36/kg** — saving Rs 3–4 per kg.

Over a year, a household consuming 10kg per month saves roughly **Rs 360–480 annually** simply by buying the larger pack.

**10kg makes sense if:**
- Your household finishes 5kg within 2–3 weeks
- You have dry, cool storage space
- The platform you use has the 10kg pack in stock at a proportional discount

Check per-kg pricing before buying — some apps price the 10kg pack disproportionately higher than two 5kg packs, particularly during sales.
