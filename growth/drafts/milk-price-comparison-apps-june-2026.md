# Cheapest Milk Prices Online: Blinkit vs Zepto vs BigBasket vs JioMart vs Instamart (June 2026)

## Which App Has the Cheapest Milk Prices Right Now?

For most Indians buying Amul milk online, **BigBasket and JioMart are your best bet**. On Amul Taaza 1L, you save ₹6 versus Instamart and ₹3 versus Blinkit and Zepto every single purchase. That adds up to ₹180+ monthly if you buy daily.

On Amul Gold 1L (full cream), BigBasket again wins at ₹69 versus Blinkit's ₹72 — a ₹3 difference per litre.

## Regional Milk Brands and Where to Buy Them Cheapest

National apps carry Amul prominently, but **regional dairy brands often beat Amul on price and freshness** in their home markets. Here is what to look for city by city.

### Nandini Milk Prices in Bangalore

Karnataka Milk Federation's **Nandini** is the dominant brand in Bangalore. Nandini Toned Milk 1L is typically priced at **₹56–₹58** on BigBasket and Blinkit in Bangalore, undercutting Amul Taaza by ₹6–₹7. For Bangaloreans, switching to Nandini is the single easiest way to lower your monthly milk bill. Nandini Gold (full cream) retails around **₹66–₹68** on quick commerce — still cheaper than Amul Gold.

Check live prices on our [Bangalore grocery prices tracker](/grocery-prices-bangalore).

### Aavin Milk Prices in Chennai

**Aavin**, run by the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Milk Producers Federation, sells toned milk at roughly **₹54–₹56 per litre** in Chennai. Availability on Blinkit and Zepto in Chennai is good; BigBasket also stocks Aavin regularly. If you are in Chennai, Aavin is almost always the cheapest option on any platform.

### Vijaya Milk Prices in Hyderabad

Andhra Pradesh Dairy Development Cooperative's **Vijaya** brand is widely available in Hyderabad on BigBasket and Blinkit. Vijaya Toned Milk 1L hovers around **₹56–₹60** depending on the platform. Vijaya Full Cream runs close to ₹66. Both beat Amul pricing in the Hyderabad market.

### Mother Dairy Prices in Delhi

**Mother Dairy** is the go-to brand for Delhi, NCR, and much of North India. Mother Dairy Toned Milk 1L is priced at **₹60–₹62** on Blinkit, Zepto, and BigBasket in Delhi — competitive with Amul Taaza. Mother Dairy Full Cream 1L costs around **₹66–₹68**. Delhi shoppers should compare Mother Dairy versus Amul on each app before checkout, as the price gap shifts frequently.

## Full Cream vs Toned vs Skimmed: Price Difference Explained

Understanding which milk type you actually need can save you money every month.

| Milk Type | Fat Content | Typical Price (1L) | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full Cream (Gold) | 6%+ | ₹69–₹72 | Children, tea, sweets |
| Standardised | 4.5% | ₹66–₹68 | General household use |
| Toned (Taaza) | 3% | ₹62–₹68 | Daily cooking, adults |
| Double Toned | 1.5% | ₹56–₹60 | Calorie-conscious adults |
| Skimmed | <0.5% | ₹54–₹57 | Weight management, protein focus |

**The price gap between full cream and toned milk is ₹7–₹10 per litre** across all apps. A household using 1 litre daily saves ₹210–₹300 per month by switching from Gold to Taaza — worth considering if you mostly use milk in tea or coffee rather than for children.
